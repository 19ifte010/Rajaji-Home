About the oraganization

Rajaji Home for the aged is a fully residential home for the poor and destitute male senior citizens. It was started in the year 1956 under the aegis of The Guild of Service, Madurai District Branch. Deserving senior citizens are admitted here irrespective of their belief, caste, creed, or religion. The Home is situated in Viswanathapuram, P & T Nagar Road, Madurai – 17.Interested inmates among them are also encouraged to maintain a small kitchen garden within the premises. Periodical medical check-ups, yoga and meditation classes are conducted to keep them hale and healthy physically and mentally. Students from the Institute of Social Sciences and other colleges visit the Home to give counseling to the needy.There are separate buildings for dwelling, dining, doing meditation, and for the conduct of meetings with all the required facilities. The Help Age India initially donated a generous sum of Rs.7,46,685/-.

Activity

The aim and object of this Home is to cater to the needs of the poor, abandoned, uncared for and homeless senior citizens who are over 60 years of age. We provide them with good food, clean clothing, a nice shelter, and give medical aid totally free of cost. To help them spend their time usefully, the inmates are provided with reading material like newspapers and magazines, and, for their entertainment a TV is also installed.The residents of this Home are happy and healthy. And for their physical activities the senior citizens can take strolls in the fenced ample open space around the Home. Interested inmates among them are also encouraged to maintain a small kitchen garden within the premises. Periodical medical check-ups, yoga and meditation classes are conducted to keep them hale and healthy physically and mentally.

Service

Our Service Associates in addition to all our services towards running the home and taking care of the elderly people to the best of our level, still we felt that we need more hands to help us. It is not about the monetary help, it is much more than that, to make our inmates of the home love the place and bringing more human touch in them.As we kept doing our services with a clear one-pointed efforts, the following Organizations volunteered to add flavor to our services:

1. Madurai Institute of Social Science

2. Sudar Foundation – A NGO

3. Sai Baba Trust

These institutions made a huge leap in our services, thereby bringing more attention to the inmates both psychologically and health wise.
